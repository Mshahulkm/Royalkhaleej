About Us

Kerala, the way it was meant to be experienced.

Royal Khaleej Holidays is a Kerala-based travel house specialising in premium leisure trips, ayurveda retreats and medical tourism for guests from the Gulf region. Every itinerary is designed by locals who know the land, the language and the little moments that make a journey unforgettable.

  • Hand-crafted itineraries — no cookie-cutter packages. Everything tailored to your pace.
  • Arabic & English speaking guides for a comfortable, familiar experience.
  • Verified premium hotels & houseboats — we've stayed in every one we recommend.
  • 24/7 on-trip support from arrival to your flight home.
